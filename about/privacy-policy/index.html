PRIVACY POLICY

The Democratizing Data project is committed to protecting the privacy and accuracy of confidential information to the extent possible, subject to provisions of state and federal law. Other than as required by laws that guarantee publkic access to certain types of information, or in response to subpoenas or other legal instruments that authorize access, personal information is not actively shared. In particula, we do not re-distribute or sell personal information collected on our web servers.

Effective Date: February 27, 2023

Updated: February 27, 2023

Information collected:

Democratizingdata.ai servers collect the following analytics:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address of computer being used
  • web pages requested
  • referring web page
  • browser used
  • date and time

Democratizing Data does not associate this data to individual user identities.

Cookies

The Democratizing Data site may use “cookies” in order to deliver web content specific to individual users’ interests. Sensitive personal information is not stored within cookies.

Webforms

The Democratizing Data site uses webforms on this site. These forms require users to give contact information including name and Email address. Contact information form the registration form is used only to send material relating to the purpose for which it was collected and will not be sold to another party.

Use of collected information:

  • Democratizing Data will only use personal information collected from this site for the purpose of communication back to individuals who contact us via the site.
  • Democratizing data uses browser-IP-address information and anonymous-browser history to report information about site accesses and for profiling purposes. This information is used to improve Web presentation and utilization. IP address information may also be used for troubleshooting purposes.

Distribution of collected information:

  • Democratizing Data will not disclose, without your consent, personal information collected about you, except for certain explicit circumstances in which disclosure is required by law.
  • Democratizing Data will not distribute or sell personal information to third-party organizations.

Privacy Statement Revisions:

We may change this Privacy Statement at any time and for any reason. We encourage you to review Privacy Statement each time you visit the web site. If we make a significant change to our Privacy Statement, we will post a notice on the homepage of our web site for a period of time after the change is made.

Responsibility for External Sites:

This website may contain links to other web sites. Some of those web sites may be operated by third parties. We provide the links for your convenience, but we do not review, control, or monitor the privacy practices of web sites operated by others.

We are not responsible for the performance of web sites operated by third parties or for your business dealings with them. Therefore, whenever you leave this website, we recommend that you review each web site’s privacy practices and make your own conclusions regarding the adequacy of these practices.

How to contact us:

If you need to contact us regarding privacy concerns, please submit an inquiry.
