The Democratizing Data platform shows the value of public data

The project is joint work with New York University, Elsevier, John Hopkins Institute for Data Intensive Engineering and Science, the Texas Advanced Computing Center, the University of Maryland, and the University of Texas at San Antonio. It developed from the Show Us the Data Kaggle competition and conference, and the Rich Context Project.
